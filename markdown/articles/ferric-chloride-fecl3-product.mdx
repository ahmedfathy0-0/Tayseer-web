## Applications of Ferric Chloride

- Ferric Chloride is a widely used coagulant in water and wastewater treatment processes, effectively clarifying and purifying water.
- Ferric Chloride is used as a coagulant in sludge thickening in sewage and industrial wastewater treatment.
- In the electronics industry, Ferric Chloride serves as a cost-effective etching solution for printed circuit boards (PCBs) in the electronic industry.

## Benefits of Ferric Chloride

- Ferric Chloride is effective in treating wastewater containing heavy metals and other contaminants.
- Ferric Chloride is a cost-efficient solution for wastewater treatment applications.
