## Calcined Alumina

TSC is a major Egyptian supplier of calcined alumina with a high concentration of aluminium oxide. TSC provides different grades and different sizes of calcined alumina for various industries. TSC aims to meet Egypt's domestic market needs.

## Applications of Calcined Alumina

- Calcined Alumina is used as a raw material in the production of aluminium products, providing the essential aluminium oxide content required for metallic aluminium production.
- In the refractory industry, Calcined Alumina serves as a crucial raw material for manufacturing high-temperature resistant materials and furnace linings.
- Calcined Alumina is utilized as a raw material in the production of ceramic glazes, enhancing the durability and appearance of ceramic products.
- It serves as a main raw material in calcium aluminate cements manufacturing, providing superior performance in specialized concrete applications.
- Calcined Alumina is used as a raw material in protective coatings, offering excellent chemical resistance and durability.
- In polishing industries, Calcined Alumina provides effective abrasive properties for various finishing applications.

## Benefits of Calcined Alumina

- Calcined Alumina offers high purity and consistent quality for demanding industrial applications.
- It provides excellent thermal stability and chemical resistance for refractory applications.
